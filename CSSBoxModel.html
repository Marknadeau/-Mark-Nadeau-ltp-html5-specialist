HTML5 Specialist Program

The HTML5 Specialist program is designed to provide developers with proof of their coding skills. The program includes a course and exam. Once you complete both the course and the exam, you will recieve the HTML5 Specialist Designation.

Upon earning the HTML5 Specialist Designation, you can display the HTML4 Specialist badge on your resume, portfolio and on your linkedin profile. You will also recieve a digidal certificate that you can print and frame.
